Betting::Betting ( )

Constructor for a betting object.

Initializes money that the player currently has. The default is 5000. Initializes betMoney (the money player places as a bet during a round) to 0. Initializes betMoneySecondHand (the money player places as a bet when their hand has been split) to 0.

◆ winMoney()

void Betting::winMoney ( )

Adds money to a player's total money when they win a round of Blackjack.

When a player wins, twice the amount that they bet is added to their total. This method also resets the betting amount for subsequent rounds.

Returns
Void.

◆ winMoneySecondHand()

void Betting::winMoneySecondHand ( )

Adds money to a player's total money in their second hand when they win a round of Blackjack.

When a player wins for their second hand, twice the amount that they bet is added to their total for their second hand. This method also resets the betting amount for the second hand for subsequent rounds.
